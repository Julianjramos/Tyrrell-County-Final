About Tyrrell County!

Tyrrell County is located in the coastal plain of northeastern North Carolina, and is mostly coastland and swamps. Tyrrell County is relatively small in population size with there only being just over 3,200 residents in 2021, but it is home to many water sports and fishing competitions due to the sheer amount of land that is uninhabited.
